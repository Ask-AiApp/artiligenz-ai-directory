About the Artiligenz Live Directory

What is this?

The Artiligenz Live Directory is a visual map of the modern AI ecosystem. Each node represents a company or product. Connections and groupings show how they relate across categories such as foundation models, assistants, creative media, data & MLOps, cloud infrastructure, and more.

How it’s organised

  • Universe view shows a full “galaxy” of AI entities, clustered into orbits and tiers (suns, planets, moons) based on their role and relative scale.
  • Grid view arranges the same entities into a clean, legible grid so you can scan names quickly and compare companies side by side.
  • Category chips (Foundation Models, Education & Learning, etc.) filter the map down to one main segment of the ecosystem at a time.
  • Search lets you jump directly to entities by name.

Who it’s for

The directory is designed for people who need a clear, high-level picture of AI:

  • Founders and product teams exploring the competitive landscape.
  • Investors and analysts tracking emerging categories and key players.
  • Researchers and practitioners looking for tools, platforms, and partners.
  • Anyone simply curious about “who does what” in the AI ecosystem.

How to read it

Each point in the map is a company or product. Colours map to high-level categories, and size roughly reflects prominence or scale. Clicking a node reveals a compact micro-card, and the “More” option opens a richer profile in the info drawer with links and additional context.

The goal isn’t to be exhaustive; it’s to be readable. The directory favours clarity over noise, so you can get oriented quickly and then drill down where it matters.
